From Shadows We Rule

1600s

The founders of Gotham erected more than buildings — they laid foundations for obedience and control. It was then the Court took root, shaping every corner of the city’s future.

1800s

Industrial titans rose, and so did their debts to the Court. The Owls directed fortunes, silenced dissenters, and curated history itself.

1900s

In the shadow of wars and madness, we remained unseen. While Gotham’s skyline reached higher, our influence ran deeper.

Present

As the world digitizes its secrets, so too has the Court adapted. Every whisper, every keystroke, every watchful camera feeds our gaze.
